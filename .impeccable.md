## Design Context

### Users
Ideathon judges and technical stakeholders evaluating a proof-of-concept. The interface must clearly demonstrate complex agentic workflow orchestration without distractions.

### Brand Personality
Clinical, stark, zero-noise, and highly intentional.

### Aesthetic Direction
Zero-noise minimal UI. A complete pivot away from the standard AI-generated dark mode dashboard (no neon, no glow, no glassmorphism). The aesthetic should rely on high contrast, strict typographic grids, significant whitespace, and severe minimalism.

### Design Principles
1. **Absolute Zero Noise**: Strip away any element that is purely decorative. No glass effects, no drop shadows, no gradient text, no colored glowing dots.
2. **Typographic Hierarchy**: Use stark contrast, scale, and distinct font choices (avoiding generic Inter/Roboto) to build hierarchy rather than relying on nested cards or colors.
3. **Raw Intentionality**: Every line, border, and spacing choice must feel hand-crafted and deliberate, completely shedding the "generic AI dashboard" aesthetic.
